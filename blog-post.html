Comets create devices to help seniors in their everyday lives

Recently the UTDesign Studio hosted its second create-a-thon following the success of the inaugural create-a-thon that took place during the fall. The event was a huge success with nearly sixty students in attendance for the twenty-six hour event. Makers were given the challenge to make something that would benefit the lives of seniors in their day to day life. The competition was fierce with the large turnout of comets. There were many great ideas and inventions created in the short time frame of twenty-six hours. The winners of the event created an easy to use pill organizer. Are you disappointed in having missed this event? No worries there are rumors that a second comets create is in the works for later this spring. So all you makers keep your ears open and your eyes on bulletin boards.
