We'd Love to Have You

At UTDesign Makerspace, we believe that everyone has a valuable skill that they can contribute. If you provide your skills, we hope can provide the tools to help you make something awesome whether that be a robot, a duct tape dress, or an amazing community.

Reach out to any one of the officers to express your interest in joining the makerspace as a member, or attend a general meeting to officially sign up to be a part of the makerspace. Don't forget to sign up for our mailing list too!

Tiers of Membership

Basic

Free
  • $0.15 3D prints (per gram)
  • Limited Open Lab Access
  • 1 Communal Storage Space
  • Limited Machinery access *
  • UTD ID for Access to Resources

Special

$1000 per semester
  • $0.05 3D prints (per gram)
  • 24/7 Lab Access via Certified Members*
  • 3 Project Storage Spaces
  • Limited Machinery access *
  • Makerspace Card benefits
  • *Will need to notify/call for after-hours service

Tool Certified Member (TCM)

Free per month
  • Free 3D Printing
  • 24/7 Lab Access
  • 3 Project Storage Space
  • Machinery access *
  • Makerspace Card benefits
  • *Must Complete 2 Bio-Raft and Machine Operation Traning Sessions

Student Toolshop Master (STM)

Free per month
  • Free 3D Printing
  • 24/7 Lab Access
  • Moderate Open Lab Space
  • 3 Project Storage Space
  • Machinery access *
  • Makerspace Card benefits
  • *Must Complete 7 Bio-Raft and Machine Operation Traning sessions
