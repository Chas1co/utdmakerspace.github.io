Teach a Class

We want you teach a class. Everyone has something they are good at, and teaching classes allows us to transfer that knowledge to others. If you have something you love, we would love to help you share it. Teach a skill that you know to everyone around you. If you teach more than three classes, we'll give you a free Google Cardboard! We want to you to utilize our space, make use of the resources.
