3D Printing

3D printing, also known as additive manufacturing (AM), refers to various processes used to synthesize a three- dimensional object. In 3D printing, successive layers of material are formed under computer control to create an object.

We have makerbots and professional grade printing services. You can print out 3D objects. Pricing usually varies by printer and demand. check out the pricing online. We offer an array 3D printers

Open Lab

If you're working on a project or a need a place to meet up, here it is! The open lab provides workspaces and tables for your groups to meet.

Community

Community. It's what we're all about. Makerspace isn't just an organization, it's a community. We are all makers, no matter what major or what we are making. From artists to architects, we are all makers of some sort.

We keep an open directory of makers so that those interested can reach out and collaborate. You can also hold sessions where you can teach people skills that you may know.

Tools List

Vertical Band Saw

A bandsaw is a power tool which uses a blade consisting of a continuous band of metal with teeth along one edge to cut various workpieces. Perfect for wood working

Drill Press

A Drill Press is a fixed style of drill that may be mounted on a stand or bolted to the floor or workbench. It can be used to drill holes in different materials

Miter Saw

A power miter saw, also known as a drop saw, is a power tool used to make a quick, accurate crosscut in a workpiece. Common uses include framing operations and the cutting of molding.

Scroll Saw

A scroll saw is a small electric or pedal-operated saw that is useful for cutting intricate curves

Bench Top Grinder

A bench grinder is a type of benchtop grinding machine used to drive abrasive wheels. A pedestal grinder is a larger version of a bench grinder that is mounted on a pedestal, which is bolted to the floor.

Assorted Hand Tools

We have tools ranging from screwdrivers, hammers to wrenches. All of the basic hand tools for your use.

Arduino Boards

Arduino's are microcontrollers that can be used to program an array of things. Ask one of the officers about renting one out

Makerbot 3D printers

3D printers that offer FDM printing for a very low-cost

Fotus 400 3D Printer

Professional grade 3D printing that can make high quality prints
